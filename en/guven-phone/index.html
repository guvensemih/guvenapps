Güven Phone

Güven Phone is a simple and accessible Android phone app developed around the needs of blind and visually impaired users.

View Güven Phone on Google Play

Key features

  • Contacts, favourites, recent calls and dial pad sections
  • Accessible controls for TalkBack and other screen readers
  • Dual-SIM support and the ability to choose which line to use
  • A straightforward call screen for answering and rejecting incoming calls
  • Spoken caller name announcements
  • Call back directly from missed-call notifications
  • Quick SMS replies and blocked-number management
  • Speaker, earpiece, mute and dial pad controls during calls

Accessibility approach

Güven Phone aims to make essential phone tasks clear and direct for people who use screen readers.

Placing calls, answering or rejecting calls, selecting contacts and using in-call controls are developed with accessibility as a priority.

Language support

Güven Phone is available in 16 languages, including Turkish.
