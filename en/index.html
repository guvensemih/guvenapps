Güven Apps is an independent app brand developing accessible and easy-to-use Android applications.

Logo description

The Güven Apps logo features a stylized letter G in dark navy and teal, shaped like a rounded shield and speech bubble. Six dots inside the symbol represent a Braille cell and accessibility.

Apps

Güven Phone

Güven Phone is an Android phone app designed to make calls, contacts, call history and in-call controls more accessible for blind and visually impaired users.

Güven Braille Keyboard

Güven Braille Keyboard is an Android keyboard app that enables Braille typing on a touchscreen and is designed for blind and visually impaired users.

Why Güven Apps?

Accessibility first

Product decisions are guided by real-device testing, user feedback and issues encountered in everyday use.
