About

Güven Apps is an independent app brand developing accessible and easy-to-use Android applications.

Who are we?

Güven Apps is a project that brings together independent Android applications developed by Semih Güven.

The primary goal of these projects is to provide simpler, more accessible and more reliable mobile experiences, especially for people who use screen readers.

What do we develop?

Güven Phone, Güven Braille Keyboard and other accessibility-focused Android projects are developed under the Güven Apps name.

The apps are developed around real user experiences, particularly the needs of blind and visually impaired Android users.

Our approach

Accessibility, straightforward use, clear interfaces and screen reader compatibility are priorities in the apps we develop.

User feedback is taken into account, and the apps continue to be improved regularly.
